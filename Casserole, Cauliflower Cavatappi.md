# Cauliflower Cavatappi Casserole

- 4-8 oz **pancetta**
- 1.5 cup **milk**
- 3 **scallions**
- ~15 oz **cauliflower florets**
- 12 oz **cavatappi** (or similar curly pasta)
- 2 tsp **chicken base concentrate**
- 3/4 cup shredded **italian cheese blend**
- 1.5 Tbsp **flour**
- 1.5 Tbsp **butter**
- **olive oil**
- **salt** to taste
- **pepper** to taste

1. Preheat oven to **400 Â°F**
1. Begin bringing a large pot of salted water to **boil**
1. Meanwhile, cut **cauliflower** head into small florets, discarding stems and rinsing in colander
1. Dry **cauliflower florets**, the toss with **olive oil** and spread on baking sheet
1. Roast **cauliflower florets** in oven until beginning to brown, **~20 min**
1. Thinly slice **scallions**, keeping greens and whites separate
1. Cook **pasta** until just before al dente (1 minute less than package instructions), then drain in colander
1. Meanwhile, brown **pancetta** in an oven-safe pan over **medium** heat
1. Add **scallion whites** just as pancetta begins to crisp
1. When **pancetta** is crisp, remove **pancetta mixture** to a small bowl with a slotted spoon, **retaining oil in the pan**
1. Melt **butter** in same pan over medium heat
1. Once melted, add **flour** and whisk constantly until it loses its raw smell, 1-2 min
1. Slowly pour in **milk**, whisking to combine
1. Stir in **chicken base** and let simmer until thickened, 1-2 min
1. Reduce heat to low and stir in **cheese**, **pancetta mixture**, and  **roasted cauliflower**
1. Season with salt and pepper to taste
1. Mix in **pasta** until well-coated
1. Bake in oven until bubbly, ~5-7 min
1. Serve and top with **scallion greens**
